Information Design: Personal Correspondence

 

I believe that writing is a way of thinking and reasoning in depth about myself and fostering my ability to explain a complex situation to myself. Naturally, the act of writing letters became a crucial activity to share my thoughts and feelings with people. This information design shows my highly personal and autobiographical data of correspondence with people in my life. With 10 components, the information design represents about when I started to write letters, what the counter-effects were, how frequently I have written letters and how the number of receivers has changed, etc. By putting the timeline in the middle, it shows how the components are interrelated. This information design shows how I utilize my old habit of writing letters.

Year : 2015
