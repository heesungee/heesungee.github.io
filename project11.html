Typography Poster Design

 

How to visualize a word, ‘press’? I started off with the question in this project. The motif of this work is a marionette puppet in order to portrait a contemporary press manipulating by the power.
By using the silhouette technique - which makes it difficult to distinguish race, gender and age - the poster will be neutral, however, at the same time, it can be read by my strong opinion in terms of contemporary press.

Summer workshop in The Basel School of Design, Switzerland

Year : 2016
Professor Leander Eisenmann
