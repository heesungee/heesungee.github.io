Map and icon design for Busan Gupo Traditional Market

 

To target young customers in their early 20’s to late 30’s, I designed Gupo Market's mobile application map to be minimalistic with iconic logos for each of the market's distinguished themed streets. Although this concept has a completely different style from the previously mentioned designs in this website, it still supports the same motif of farmers' effort and their sweat.

Year : 2014
