Memory remains on the folding line

 

I remembered the way I folded handwritten-letters when I exchanged them with friends in middle school without using envelopes. I borrowed that method, and I came up with the new composition using the folding lines. When I unfolded them, they were really crumpled, but I loved the folding lines, creases, and the way I unfold them. The receiver can read the contents in order as they open the hand-written letters. In this letterform project, where I combined hand-drawn letterforms, and a rigid and a tight set of parameters.

Year: 2015
