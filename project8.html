Tissue Box Packaging Design

 

The Save the Environment Illustration campaign is a design project to bring awareness to the disappearing habitat of wildlife in nature. For this campaign, I drew two animal species illustrations: one living in the North Pole and one in a woodland habitat. The two versions are designed as a limited edition tissue box. It has the slogan ‘HELP SAVE WILDLIFE.’ The animals on the tissue box are metaphorically asking for help with their sad and long face.

Year : 2011
Professor Sujin Park
