Bootleg Printed Publication

 

In a publication, I was asked to develop a publication that can be a bootleg in transduction by using any materials. With an idea of creating a bootleg publication version of a film titled Memento, directed by Christopher Nolan in 2001, I did screenshots of each representative scene from the movie and analyzed the movie script.
