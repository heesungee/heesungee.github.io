Eat Less Salt Campaign

 

Most people eat too much salt without even realizing it. Although we may only add a small amount of salt to our food at home, about 75% of the recommended salt intake is already in the food we buy -- making the overconsumption of salt a serious problem worldwide. Therefore, to encourage the reduction of salt intake, I theorized that communicating this message using illustrations is the best way to do it since illustrations are far louder than words.
