Externalization versus Internalization
Introvert versus Extrovert personality
Being literal versus Poetic
Direct versus Indirect

This project centers around the use of indirect, or euphemistic, visual language and circumlocution, in combination with every day, personal objects. My project uses every day, personal objects as memory triggers that represent my identity and perspective. I’m interested in understanding the way metaphor changes the way we understand these ordinary objects. I photographed everyday objects taken with me from Korea to the US, which serve as touchstones; means of portraying myself as projected onto objects.
